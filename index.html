About

I got into content marketing because I like writing, and I stayed because I love helping brands get discovered online.

Today, I'm the Director of Marketing at Siege Media, where I lead our content strategy, oversee editorial, manage a team of writers, and support business development through thought leadership, customer stories, data studies, and other marketing initiatives. As AI reshaped search, I helped lead our transition from an SEO-focused agency to a full-service GEO agency by developing our positioning, launching new service pages, aligning internal teams, and helping clients navigate the shift.

These days, I'm especially interested in the intersection of content marketing, SEO, and AI search, and how brands can build lasting visibility as the way people search continues to evolve.

Before content took over, I was a graphic designer creating logos, apparel, and hyperspecific memes for whatever group chat I was in. While design isn't my full-time job anymore, I'm still your guy for a quick T-shirt, presentation, or social graphic.

Over the past five years, I've helped startups and Fortune 500 companies grow through content, SEO, and AI search. I enjoy building content programs from the ground up, turning complex topics into something people actually want to read, and finding creative ways to solve marketing challenges.

When I'm off the clock, you'll usually find me hanging out with friends, biking around NYC, running, or listening to as much music as humanly possible. If anything here resonated with you, I'd love to connect, whether that's over Zoom, a cup of coffee, or a beer.
